Our Solutions
What is a Tennis Central Pod?

A Tennis Central Pod is a light tennis unit that offers full-fledged tennis activities, programs, and events without the heavy corporate requirements of a tennis club or country club setting. If you have access to courts and a community, you have a perfect setup for a Tennis Central Pod. It is all about ease of access, convenience, and quality service at the right price point.

Tennis X Certification

If you are an intermediate or advanced tennis player who loves the game and would like to turn your tennis skills into a cash machine, welcome! With Tennis X Certification, you will elevate your status and put your tennis business in hyper drive. To learn more, please see our short Turn Your Tennis Skills Into a Cash Machine video.

About Us
History

We are a family-oriented corporation. Our #1 goal is to make you successful beyond your wildest dreams and free your time.

It started as a dare and morphed into an industry. A tennis establishment executive I looked up to once told me that I should stick to teaching and leave the business side to him. I dared myself to produce products and services that will revolutionize the way the game is taught, played, and accessed. If you dream of a better way, build it.

Tennis Central Corp is the gateway for all of us who know tennis has not seen its brightest years yet and are ready to do something about it—join us and disrupt.

Our Philosophy

Mobility and real-time communication will make you thrive. People love tennis—give them fast and easy access. Traditions are good, but not at the expense of our amazing enthusiasts. Which events would you rather attend?

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower the next generation of tennis bosses and tennis players. Times have changed. It's time to upgrade!
